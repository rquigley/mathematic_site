Performance, Security and Scaling

In this day and age, there are no excuses for slow-loading websites. We start with analysis of your existing infrastructure and application codebase and quickly set up business and/or system instrumentation to find bottlenecks. We collect, analyze, and then propose potential solutions with full justification, benefits and rollout strategy.

No site is completely secure, but using best-practices, you can mitigate that risk. We can audit your codebase and infrastructure and propose fixes that keep your users safe and your data secure.
