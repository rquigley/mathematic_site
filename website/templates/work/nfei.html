The North Fork Education Initiative (NFEI) is a new nonprofit on the North Fork of Long Island whose mission is to create educational programs and opportunities with a focus on arts, environment and community.

Mathematic was brought on for strategy, design and development.

Tooling used:

  • Symfony2 PHP Framework
  • Mathematic CMS adminstration system
  • Sass for modularization of stylesheets and to ease cross-browser development.
  • Stripe for donation processing and event registration
