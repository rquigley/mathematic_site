Galleray is a web application designed to showcase and sell artwork. Artists have the ability to set up galleries and to promote individual works within the Galleray community. Visitors can curate their own exhibitions and purchase favorite works. In limited beta, the site plans on launching later this year

Mathematic was hired by 3atmospheres for front-end development and later in the project, back-end development. 3atmospheres handled back-end development and project management and Gin Lane Media was hired for design.

Tooling used:

  • Sass for modularization of stylesheets and to ease cross-browser development.
  • Django Python Framework
